About Me

I graduated at Aklan State University - Kalibo, Aklan with the degree of Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, Batch 2012.

I started my career with passion and commitment to seek knowledge to improve myself in this profession and offer what I can do. Strongly tackle this line of vocation to prove that I'm worthy and fit being a software developer.

Being a programmer is inevitable stressful and never-ending series of problem solving that sometimes it's like "being a detective in a crime that unconsciously you are also the murderer". Every challenge I have experienced trying to force me to give up, nonetheless, when I accomplished my goal even though it's hard, fulfillment lift up the feeling that I can do much more out of my best I could possibly do. I always go with the flow of IT Innovations by improving the range of my skill sets so that I can easily adapt any technologies.
